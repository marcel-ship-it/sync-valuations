01 / What Already Works
THE VISUAL TONE IS RIGHT
Warm, editorial, confident. Not cold-corporate, not startup-y. The cream + navy + condensed bold type combination IS Sync Valuations. We keep all of this.
Warm Cream Background

Not white, not beige — a specific warm cream that feels premium and distinctive. Sets SV apart from every other legal-adjacent service.

Keep
Bold Condensed Headlines

Uppercase, heavy weight, tight tracking. Commands attention without being aggressive. Very editorial — like a quality broadsheet.

Keep
Deep Navy as Primary

Not black — a specific deep navy (#1B2C3C) that's authoritative but warmer than pure black. Works beautifully on cream.

Keep
Monospace Labels

DELIVERABLES, METHODOLOGY, INQUIRY — uppercase monospace section labels give structure and a technical, precise feel.

Keep
Geometric Shapes

The navy + khaki + gray geometric composition in the hero. Abstract but grounded. No stock photos, no illustrations — pure graphic authority.

Keep
Generous Whitespace

Big breathing room between sections. Nothing feels cramped. The space itself communicates confidence and premium positioning.

Keep
02 / What to Improve
STRUCTURE &
TECHNICAL QUALITY
The visual tone stays. The underlying structure needs a proper rebuild — semantic HTML, SEO, responsive design, better information architecture.
Semantic HTML & SEO

Proper meta tags, Open Graph, structured data, semantic headings (h1 → h6), alt text, canonical URLs. Currently missing — built in Google AI Studio.

Add
Mobile Responsiveness

Full responsive layout. The current site likely breaks on mobile. Hero text, three-column cards, and navigation need mobile treatment.

Add
Stats & Social Proof

Add a stats bar (73% settlement rate, 40+ years combined, 3 jurisdictions) and real client testimonials. Currently only has one internal quote.

Add
Team Section

Currently just names + email/LinkedIn. Needs proper bios showing 20+ years experience each, specific expertise, credibility markers.

Improve
Scroll Animations

Subtle fade-in and slide-up on section entry. The current site is static — adding light motion will make it feel polished.

Add
Footer & Legal

Proper footer with "A Ringo company", privacy/terms links, structured contact info. Currently minimal.

Improve
Performance

Optimized loading, minimal dependencies, proper font loading strategy. No heavy frameworks — clean HTML/CSS/JS.

Add
Copy Updates (Brand Voice)

Apply the brand voice guidelines: correct facts (20+ years each), proper product naming (Sync Valuation Report), testimony framing.

Improve
