The Challenge You Face

Your client's music was used without authorization. You need to establish what the sync license should have cost — but sync licensing has no published rate card, no central database of prices, and wildly varying benchmarks depending on who you ask. The opposing side will challenge any number you put forward.

You need an independent expert who can produce a valuation that holds up under cross-examination. Not a guess. Not a rule of thumb. A structured, evidence-based analysis backed by verifiable data and a proven methodology.
