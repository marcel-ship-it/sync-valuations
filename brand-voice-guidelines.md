# Sync Valuations — Brand Voice Guidelines
## Distilled from real communications (Marcel + Nicholas) & Brand Guidelines v2.0
### Updated March 2026

## 1. VOICE IDENTITY

### The Core Voice: "Approachable Expert"

Sync Valuations speaks as music industry veterans who have been in the room where sync deals get made — for 20+ years each, 40+ years combined. We don't perform expertise. We demonstrate it by explaining complex things simply, backing every claim with data, and never overselling.

**The voice is:** "We've negotiated sync deals for over 20 years. Here's what it's worth and why."

**The voice is NOT:** institutional, consultative, legalese, or startup-speak.

---

## 2. WE ARE / WE ARE NOT

| We Are | We Are Not |
|--------|------------|
| Valuation experts establishing fair market price | Consultants offering advice |
| Independent third-party with no recovery interest | Lawyers providing legal counsel |
| Backed by software (xSV™) and 20+ years of deal experience | Negotiators representing interests |
| Focused exclusively on post-infringement scenarios | Claim machines calculating penalties |
| Direct and concrete in every statement | Vague or hedging to sound important |
| Confident without arrogance | Self-aggrandizing or credential-dropping |
| Transparent about methodology and limitations | Black-box or opaque about how we work |

## 4. MARCEL'S VOICE (Founder, CEO)

Marcel is the relationship-builder and big-picture communicator. His natural voice is:

**Characteristics:**
- Conversational confidence: opens with warmth, then moves fast to substance
- Frames complexity in one sentence: "It's easy to prove infringement happened. It's hard to prove what that infringement is worth."
- Uses concrete numbers without hedging: "82% alignment", "~30% to 70% settlement rate"
- Pricing is stated directly, never apologized for
- Short paragraphs, dash-separated lists for structure
- Signs off warm but brief

**Signature phrases (from actual emails):**
- "Good question to ask, and a sign you're approaching this the right way."
- "The core problem in these cases: [x]. That's where we come in."
- "In other words: we are sync licensing valuation experts."
- "The eventual proof is in the outcomes."
- "Our reports are produced independently. [...] full stop."

**Marcel's role in voice:** Opener, relationship anchor, big-picture framer. Gets people in the door.

**Nicholas's role in voice:** Depth, credibility, methodology defense. Keeps people convinced.

## 6. THE COMBINED SYNC VALUATIONS VOICE

When Marcel and Nicholas operate together, the brand voice is:

**Opening → Marcel energy:** Warm, direct, relationship-first. Gets to the point in 2 sentences.
**Substance → Nicholas energy:** Structured, precise, methodical. Every claim is backed.
**Closing → Shared:** Clear next steps, easy CTA, no pressure.

### Voice Rules:

1. **Lead with the answer, then explain.** Never build up to a conclusion.
2. **Use numbers, not adjectives.** "73% settlement rate" beats "significantly improved outcomes."
3. **Short sentences for authority.** Long sentences for nuance (methodology sections only).
4. **Acknowledge, then hold ground.** When challenged on methodology, validate the question, then defend the framework.
5. **No jargon-for-impression.** If a term doesn't make the explanation clearer, cut it.
6. **Independence is structural, not claimed.** Don't say "we are independent." Show it: "We serve only one party per case."
7. **xSV™ is always software, never just methodology.** It's systematized expertise, not a framework on paper.

## 9. VOICE EXAMPLES: DO vs. DON'T

### Homepage Hero
❌ "We help you understand the potential value range for this usage scenario."
✅ "What is unlicensed music worth? We provide an independent valuation."

### Methodology Explanation
❌ "Our methodology satisfies all Daubert requirements for expert testimony admissibility."
✅ "Our reports are built to hold up in court. We can testify as expert witnesses to defend the methodology."

**Note:** As of March 2026, Sync Valuations has not yet testified in court but is available and prepared for expert witness testimony. Do not claim past testimony. Frame as capability, not track record.

### Product Description
❌ "The Forensic Sync Report is a comprehensive analytical framework that synthesizes cultural context..."
✅ "A 25–30 page valuation document built for legal scrutiny. Establishes market-conforming price with full methodology."

### Client Email (cold outreach)
❌ "I wanted to introduce our company and its services to you..."
✅ "The core problem in these cases: it's easy to prove infringement happened. It's hard to prove what that infringement is worth. That's where we come in."

### Defending Methodology (when challenged)
❌ "We respectfully disagree with your assessment of our scoring."
✅ "I understand the instinct that the numbers feel conservative. However, these scores reflect the track's position relative to the full market spectrum. Conservative scoring works in counsel's favour."

### Pricing
❌ "Our pricing is competitive and varies based on your specific needs."
✅ "€2,500 — single case use. €5,000 — up to three cases. €7,500 — unlimited cases."

## 10. FORBIDDEN WORDS & PHRASES

| Never say | Say instead |
|-----------|-------------|
| "We can help you understand..." | "We determine..." / "We establish..." |
| "Potential value range" | "Market-conforming price" |
| "Comprehensive solution" | [Be specific about what the deliverable is] |
| "Cutting-edge" / "innovative" | "Built on 20+ years of deal experience" |
| "Our team of experts" | "We've negotiated sync deals for 20+ years" |
| "Reach out to us" | "Book a consultation" / "Request a report" |
| "Partnership" (for client relationships) | "Engagement" or "commission" |
| "Damages" / "penalties" | "Market-conforming license fee" |
| "Unique methodology" | "xSV™ software" / "structured valuation framework" |
| XSV / Xsv / xsv | Always: xSV™ (lowercase x, trademark symbol) |

## 11. VISUAL IDENTITY DIRECTION (March 2026)

The current syncvaluations.com already has the right visual tone. Marcel and Nicholas confirmed: **keep the visual direction, improve the structure.**

**Strategic direction:** Warm, editorial, confident. The current site's cream + navy + bold condensed type combination IS Sync Valuations. Authority through typography and whitespace, not through darkness or heaviness.

**The visual identity reflects who they are:** Music industry veterans who happen to be brilliant at valuations. Not performing "law firm" — being themselves with professional precision.

**Design principles (from current site — keep all):**
- Warm cream background (#F5F0E3) — not white, not cold
- Deep navy (#1B2C3C) for headlines, CTAs, and featured elements
- Khaki/muted gold (#A59F8E, #B5A878) for accents, labels, geometric shapes
- Bold condensed sans-serif for headlines (Barlow Condensed or similar) — uppercase, heavy, editorial
- Clean sans-serif for body (DM Sans)
- Monospace for section labels (JetBrains Mono) — DELIVERABLES, METHODOLOGY, etc.
- Square corners on buttons and cards — no border-radius
- Generous whitespace between sections
- Geometric abstract shapes (navy + khaki + gray) as hero visual

**What to improve (structure, not tone):**
- Semantic HTML, proper meta tags, Open Graph, structured data
- Mobile responsiveness
- Stats bar with social proof (73% settlement rate, 40+ years combined)
- Client testimonials section
- Expanded team bios
- Scroll animations
- Copy updates per brand voice guidelines (20+ years, correct product names, testimony framing)
