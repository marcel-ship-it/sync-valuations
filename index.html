The Question Has Shifted

The Question
Has Shifted

Music is embedded across digital media, from reels and shorts to branded posts and livestream clips. In many cases, usage is visible and established, but the economic meaning of that usage remains unclear.

The question is no longer whether music was used, but what that use would have cost if it had been licensed properly.

"For a long time, there was no independent way to calculate that value consistently. We developed a valuation framework to determine the equivalent sync value of that usage, grounded in real-world licensing benchmarks and market logic."

Methodology

How We
Determine Value

We apply xSV™ (Expected Sync Value), our valuation framework underlying every Sync Valuation Report and Valuation Matrix. It combines historical sync benchmarks with cultural context and real-time usage signals to model defensible valuation ranges, grounded in observable data and professional judgement shaped by over 20 years of sync licensing practice.
